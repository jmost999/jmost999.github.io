  3. Tennis

As mentioned in the first page I played tennis in high school- I really enjoyed it but am ready to move onto new challenges. While I have not played in a while I certainly miss the sport and hopefully one day will be able to return (pun intended!) to it. At the fourth spot I played basketball. I am not very good at all- but so are the people I normally play with! I enjoy basketball because of how many people can play at once and how easily accessible it is. I also enjoy the teamwork and the intricacy of running certain plays.
